# Startup Idea Evaluation Template

Use this template to think through a startup idea honestly.

## 1. Idea name

Write the idea in one line.

## 2. Who is this for?

Describe the specific user.

## 3. What problem does it solve?

Write the problem simply and clearly.

## 4. How painful is the problem?

- Low
- Medium
- High

Why?

## 5. How do people solve it today?

List current alternatives.

## 6. Why are current solutions not enough?

Be specific.

## 7. What is the simplest possible solution?

Describe the smallest version worth testing.

## 8. Why would someone care?

What is the real benefit?

## 9. What evidence do I have?

Examples:

- Interviews
- Signups
- Requests
- Market behavior
- Personal experience

## 10. What would invalidate this idea?

Write what would prove this is weak.

## 11. What is the next test?

Choose one real-world validation step.

## Rule

Do not use this template to convince yourself the idea is good.

Use it to find where it is weak.
